【课程】03 Richards方程数值解

发表于2020-12-22|更新于2020-12-30|课程
|字数总计:2.4k|阅读时长:11分钟|阅读量:|评论数:

1 模型结构

1.1 Richards方程

$Z$方向Richards方程
$$
\frac{\partial \theta}{\partial t} = \frac{\partial}{\partial z}[D(\theta) \frac{\partial \theta}{\partial z} - K(\theta)]
$$

式中，$D(\theta)$为扩散率，$K(\theta)$为导水率

1.2 差分格式

$$
\frac{\partial \theta}{\partial z} = \frac{\theta_{i+\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1}-\theta_{i-\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1}}{\Delta z}
$$

$$ \frac{\partial \theta}{\partial t} = \frac{\theta_{i}^{j+1}-\theta_{i}^{j}}{\Delta t} $$

1.3 方程离散

$$ \frac{\theta_{i}^{j+1}-\theta_{i}^{j}}{\Delta t} = \frac{[D(\theta) \frac{\partial \theta}{\partial z} - K(\theta)]_{i+\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1} - [D(\theta) \frac{\partial \theta}{\partial z} - K(\theta)]_{i-\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1}}{\Delta z} $$

$$ [D(\theta) \frac{\partial \theta}{\partial z} - K(\theta)]_{i+\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1} = D(\theta)_{i+\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1} (\frac{\theta_{i+\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1}-\theta_{i-\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1}}{\Delta z})_{i+\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1}- K(\theta)_{i+\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1} = D(\theta)_{i+\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1} \frac{\theta_{i+1}^{j+1}-\theta_{i}^{j+1}}{\Delta z}- K(\theta)_{i+\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1} $$

$$ [D(\theta) \frac{\partial \theta}{\partial z} - K(\theta)]_{i-\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1} = D(\theta)_{i-\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1} (\frac{\theta_{i+\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1}-\theta_{i-\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1}}{\Delta z})_{i-\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1}- K(\theta)_{i-\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1} = D(\theta)_{i-\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1} \frac{\theta_{i}^{j+1}-\theta_{i-1}^{j+1}}{\Delta z}- K(\theta)_{i-\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1} $$

则
$$ \frac{\theta_{i}^{j+1}-\theta_{i}^{j}}{\Delta t} = \frac{1}{\Delta z}[D(\theta)_{i+\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1} \frac{\theta_{i+1}^{j+1}-\theta_{i}^{j+1}}{\Delta z}- K(\theta)_{i+\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1}-D(\theta)_{i-\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1} \frac{\theta_{i}^{j+1}-\theta_{i-1}^{j+1}}{\Delta z}+ K(\theta)_{i-\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1}] $$

$$ \frac{\Delta t D(\theta)_{i-\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1}}{{\Delta z}^2}\theta_{i-1}^{j+1}+\{1+\frac{\Delta t}{{\Delta z}^2}[D(\theta)_{i+\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1}-D(\theta)_{i-\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1}]\}\theta_i^{j+1}-\frac{\Delta t D(\theta)_{i+\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1}}{{\Delta z}^2}\theta_{i+1}^{j+1} = \theta_i^j-\frac{\Delta t}{\Delta z}K(\theta)_{i+\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1}+\frac{\Delta t}{\Delta z}K(\theta)_{i-\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1} $$

即
$$ A_i\theta_{i-1}^{j+1} + B_i\theta_{i}^{j+1}+C_i\theta_{i+1}^{j+1}=D_i $$
式中
$$ A_i = \frac{\Delta t }{{\Delta z}^2}D(\theta)_{i-\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1} $$

$$ B_i = 1+\frac{\Delta t}{{\Delta z}^2}[D(\theta)_{i+\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1}-D(\theta)_{i-\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1}] $$

$$ C_i = -\frac{\Delta t }{{\Delta z}^2}D(\theta)_{i+\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1} $$

$$ D_i = \theta_i^j-\frac{\Delta t}{\Delta z}[K(\theta)_{i+\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1}-K(\theta)_{i-\frac{1}{2}}^{j+1}] $$

采用半隐式解法，用$j$时刻的$D$和$K$代替$j+1$时刻的值，采用上游迁移法或
$$
K_{i \pm \frac{1}{2}}=\frac{K_i+K_{i\pm1}}{2}
$$

$$
D_{i \pm \frac{1}{2}}=\frac{D_i+D_{i\pm1}}{2}
$$

2 模型条件

2.1 边界条件

上下边界可以是土壤含水率固定值或时间序列$\theta(t)$，在三对角矩阵的第一行和最后一行添加。

2.2 初始条件

$$
\theta_x^0 = \theta_0
$$

2.3 $K(\theta)$

非饱和土壤导水率$K$与土壤水吸力$s$、或与土壤含水率$\theta$的关系，常根据实测结果拟合为经验公式。常用的经验公式形式有
$$
K(\theta) = K_t(\frac{\theta}{\theta_s})^m 或 K(\theta)=K_t(\frac{\theta - \theta_0}{\theta_s - \theta_0})^m
$$

$$
K(\theta) = K_t e^{-\beta(\theta_s-\theta)}
$$

式中，$K_t$为饱和导水率，$\theta_s$为饱和含水率，$\theta_0$为某一特征含水率，$m$为经验常数。

2.4 $D(\theta)$

定义非饱和土壤水的扩散率$D(\theta)$为导水率$K(\theta)$和比水容量$C(\theta)$的比值，即
$$
D(\theta)=\frac{K(\theta)}{C(\theta)}=K(\theta)/\frac{d\theta}{d\psi_m}
$$
显然，非饱和土壤水的扩散率$D$同样是土壤含水率$\theta$的或基质势$\psi_m$的函数。其函数关系必须通过试验测定，常用的经验公式形式有
$$
D(\theta) = ae^{b\theta}
$$

$$
D(\theta) = D_0(\frac{\theta}{\theta_s})^m
$$

$$
D(\theta) = D_0e^{-\beta(\theta_0-\theta)}
$$

式中，$\theta_0$为某一特征含水率，$a$，$b$，$D_0$、$m$、$\beta$为经验常数，取决于土壤质地和结构。

3 模型求解

3.1 三对角矩阵

三对角矩阵$Ax=B$形式如下：
$$ \underbrace{\begin{pmatrix} 1&0&0&0&\dots&\dots&\dots&\dots&\dots&\dots&0\\[4pt] A_{2}&B_{2}&C_{2}\\[4pt] & A_{3}&B_{3}&C_{3}\\[4pt] & & A_{4}&B_{4}&C_{4} \\[4pt] \\[4pt] & & &&&&&&\\[4pt] & & &&&&&&\\[4pt] & & &&&&&&\\[4pt] & & &&&&&&\\[4pt] & &&&&&& A_{n-2}&B_{n-2}&C_{n-2} \\[4pt] & & &&&&&&A_{n-1}&B_{n-1}&C_{n-1} \\[4pt] 0&\dots &\dots &\dots&\dots&\dots&\dots&0 & 0 &0 &1 \\[4pt] \end{pmatrix}}_{A} \underbrace{\begin{pmatrix} \theta_{1} \\[4pt] \theta_{2} \\[4pt] \theta_{3} \\[4pt] \theta_{4} \\[4pt] \theta_{5} \\[4pt] \\[4pt] \\[4pt] \\[4pt] \\[4pt] \theta_{n-2} \\[4pt] \theta_{n-1} \\[4pt] \theta_{n} \\[4pt] \end{pmatrix}}_{x}=\underbrace{\begin{pmatrix} \theta_{1}(t) \\[4pt] D_{2} \\[4pt] D_{3} \\[4pt] D_{4} \\[4pt] D_{5} \\[4pt] \\[4pt] \\[4pt] \\[4pt] \\[4pt] D_{n-2} \\[4pt] D_{n-1} \\[4pt] \theta_{n}(t) \\[4pt] \end{pmatrix}}_{B} $$

参数 含义 取值
